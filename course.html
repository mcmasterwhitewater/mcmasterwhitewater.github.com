Courses

As promised, yes we do indeed offer several courses throughout the year

These courses range from a beginner course for those with little to no experience

to a roll clinic for intermediate kayakers who want to perfect their roll

Sign up at the McMaster Athletics Web site for the courses listed below:


Two Beginner Courses are Currently Being Offered:

Fall 2010 November 6-7, 13-14 3-5pm
Winter 2011: March 5-6 & March 12-13 3-5pm

One Roll Clinic is Currently Being Offered:

March 19-20, 2011

Also we have open sessions on Fridays from 8:30-9:30pm. Open Sessions will start September 24th, 2010 and run until Mid March 2011.

Online registration is not available for the open sessions, please visit the pool in the Ivor Wynne building during the open session time to register.

The cost Cost for semester 2 is $30 for students, $40 for non-students. Don't know if kayaking will be for you? Come out and enjoy the first pool session for free and check things out.

The club has a kayaks and skirts available for members (and curious newbies) that do not possess a boat. You only need to bring a swimsuit and a towel.

If you have any questions email mcmasterwhitewater@gmail.com

See you on the river (or in the pool).
